Train Types

  • Rajdhani Express: Fastest trains connecting major cities to the capital.
  • Shatabdi Express: Daytime intercity trains with comfortable seating.
  • Garib Rath: Affordable air-conditioned trains for budget travelers.
  • Duronto Express: Non-stop long-distance trains for quick travel.
  • Humsafar Express: Premium trains with modern amenities for long journeys.

Booking Information

  1. Tickets can be booked online through the official railway website or mobile app.
  2. Advance booking is recommended, especially during peak travel seasons.
  3. Different classes of travel are available, including Sleeper, AC 3-tier, AC 2-tier, and First Class.
  4. Passengers are advised to carry valid identification during travel.
  5. Check the train schedule and platform information before departure.
